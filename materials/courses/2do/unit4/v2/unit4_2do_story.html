🏙️ A Day in My City
A story about places in town

Today, I go to the city with my family.

First, we go to the supermarket. 🛒 Mom buys fruits and vegetables there.

Then, Dad puts gas in the car at the gas station. ⛽

After that, we go to the park. 🌳 I play with my friends and we run and jump!

In the afternoon, we eat pizza at the restaurant. 🍕 It is very delicious!

At night, we see a movie at the movie theater. 🎬 I love my city! ❤️

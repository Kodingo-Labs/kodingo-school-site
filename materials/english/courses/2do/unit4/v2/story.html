🏙️ Lima — My Best City!

My name is Mateo and I live in Lima, my best city ever! 🌟

In my city there is a beautiful park. I love to play there with my friends.

My mom goes to the supermarket to buy fruits and vegetables for dinner.

My dad works at the bank. He helps people save their money.

On Fridays, our family goes to the restaurant to eat pizza. Yummy! 🍕

My teacher works at my school. She is very kind and funny.

I want to be a doctor when I grow up and help people at the hospital!
